Crime Prediction of Indore City



This is a ML based flask application which is designed to predict the type of crime

that might occur at a specific location(In Indore city) at specific point of time.



We predict the output with inputs like data,time and location

The output can be any one of the following cases..

  • Robbery(Act-379)
  • Gambling(Act-13)
  • Accident(Act-279)
  • Violence(Act-323)
  • Murder(Act-302)
  • Kidnapping(Act-363)
